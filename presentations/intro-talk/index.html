TaskCluster

Design Goals

  • Keep configurations in-tree,
    • payload: {
  image:    'custom-docker-image',
  command:  '...'
}, ...
  • Support different WorkerTypes,
    • workerType: 'docker-worker',
...
  • High availability and scalability
